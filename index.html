How many times did you go to a museum during your travel and were irritated by high admission fees, long waiting lines and crowds that do not let you concentrate on immortal art exhibited there?

How many times did you wish you would travel and visit famous museums but could not because you were too busy? Or because travel became too expensive? Or simply because travel is difficult? Just think about booking tickets and hotels, dragging luggage around, arguing with taxi drivers and waiters who do not know your language and worrying about pickpockets and exchange rates.

Still, you would like to see all those famous paintings.

Museum Visitr is the solution to all these problems.

Museum Visitr will collect images of famous paintings from the best museums for you. You will be able to browse through these beautiful pictures free of charge.

For a nominal fee, Museum Visitr will give you "guided tours" through these collections, describing the artists, their place in art history and pointing to important techniques that they used.

Even if you do travel to these far-away destinations, Museum Visitr will help you to prepare for your trip - it will tell you what you will see and why you should enjoy it. And after you return, Museum Visitr will help you refresh your memories of the trip and learn more about paintings that you missed.
